Tizzy Twist

A playful and vibrant brand identity project created for a fun, energetic water park concept, designed to capture excitement through bold visuals, colorful branding, and memorable promotional design.

View Case Study Explore More Projects

Overview

Tizzy Twist was developed as a brand identity project for a water park experience aimed at feeling fun, youthful, and full of energy. The project focused on creating a visual system that could instantly convey excitement, movement, and a playful atmosphere while remaining visually cohesive across brand materials.

Challenge

The main challenge was to create a brand that felt lively and highly memorable without becoming visually chaotic. The identity needed to balance bright colors, bold shapes, and playful typography in a way that still looked polished, consistent, and suitable for promotional and digital use.

Solution

I designed a cheerful and high-energy visual identity using vibrant colors, dynamic graphic elements, and a strong logo direction that reflected the playful nature of the brand. The final concept helped position Tizzy Twist as a fun and engaging destination with a clear and recognizable visual personality.

Brand Direction

The project explored colorful branding, promotional design, and a youthful visual tone to create a brand system that could work across posters, social media, signage, and merchandise. The direction was built to feel energetic, fresh, and instantly eye-catching.

Tools

The project used graphic design tools and branding principles to build a consistent and visually engaging identity system.
