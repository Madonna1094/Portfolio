UI/UX Case Study

Budget Bites

A mobile-first meal planning and budgeting app concept designed to help users manage grocery spending, organize weekly meals, and make smarter food choices through a simple and user-friendly interface.

View Case Study Explore More Projects

Overview

Budget Bites was created as a UX-focused project to solve a common everyday problem: planning meals while staying within a budget. The concept combines meal organization, grocery planning, and cost awareness in one experience to support users who want affordable and practical food decisions without feeling overwhelmed.

Challenge

Many users struggle to balance meal planning, grocery costs, and time management in their daily routines. The challenge was to design an interface that made budgeting feel approachable while keeping the app easy to navigate, visually clean, and useful for real-life meal planning habits.

Solution

I designed a structured mobile app experience with clear navigation, simple budgeting flows, and an organized visual hierarchy. The solution focused on helping users browse meal ideas, plan weekly meals, estimate grocery costs, and stay in control of spending through an intuitive and engaging interface.

Platform

The project was designed as a mobile app concept in Figma, with attention to responsive thinking, usability, and clean interaction design. The interface was built to feel modern, practical, and easy to understand for everyday users.

Technologies

The project combined UX strategy, interface design, and prototyping tools to create a polished digital product concept.
